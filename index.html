About

Recently graduated with BA degree in English Literature, Film, and a certificate in Local Food Systems. Ten years of experience in hospitality and food service industry. Additional experience in organizing events, managing calendars and coordinating groups. Strong customer service and organizational skills. Ability to learn concepts quickly. Endless curiosity and desire to improve. Good communication and work ability in fast-paced environments. I like to have a lot to do.

I have made sure to work for places where quality is a passion because I believe that a good product can be revolutionary. I am very experienced in the restaurant industry. Food is a personal passion of mine and I have tried to gain experience in as many aspects of this industry as possible. To me food is a special type of relationship and a restaurant with integrity has incredible influence on that relationship. I want to work somewhere that is inspired by the dynamics at work in this part of human culture and I am trying to find work with you because I feel I have something to learn from your process.
